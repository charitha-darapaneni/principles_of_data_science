The dataset consists of the following attributes 

name of the cars, 
location, 
year - year of manufacture
kilometers_driven,
fuel type, 
Transmission, 
owner type,
mileage,
engine - displacement
power - engine horse power
seats - number of seats
new_price - when the car was newly bought, measured in lakhs
price attributes - target variable, measured in lakhs

Price is the target variable.
